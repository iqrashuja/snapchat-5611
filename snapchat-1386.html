Staying Safe on Snapchat

Keeping your personal information private on Snapchat is crucial. Never share sensitive details like your full name, address, or phone number. Exercise caution in adding friends and only accept requests from individuals you know. Regularly review your privacy settings to ensure they are set to your comfort level. Remember, once you share a snap or story, it can be saved or shared beyond your control.

Avoid engaging with strangers and report any suspicious activity immediately. In case of cyberbullying or harassment, block the user and report the behavior without delay. Encourage open communication with your parents or guardians about your Snapchat usage. By staying vigilant and following these safety tips, you can enjoy Snapchat while keeping yourself protected.
